Crude Distillation Unit (CDU)

The Crude Distillation Unit is the first major process in a petroleum refinery. It separates crude oil into fractions by heating and then condensing the vapor.

Key Operating Parameters:

  • Temperature: 350°C to 400°C
  • Pressure: Slightly above atmospheric
  • Main products: Naphtha, Kerosene, Diesel, Residue

This process is essential for feedstock preparation for further refining.
