Crude Distillation Unit (CDU)

The Crude Distillation Unit (CDU) is the first major unit in a petroleum refinery. It separates crude oil into various fractions by distillation under atmospheric pressure.

  • Operating Temp: 350°C–400°C
  • Pressure: Atmospheric
  • Products: Naphtha, Kerosene, Diesel, Gas Oil, Residue

CDU serves as the foundation for downstream processes such as reforming, cracking, and hydroprocessing.
