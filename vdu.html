Vacuum Distillation Unit (VDU)

The Vacuum Distillation Unit (VDU) processes the heavy residue from the CDU. It operates under vacuum to prevent thermal cracking and to recover valuable gas oils and lubricants.

  • Operating Temp: ~400°C
  • Pressure: 30–50 mmHg (vacuum)
  • Products: Light vacuum gas oil, heavy vacuum gas oil, vacuum residue

The VDU is essential for maximizing yield and minimizing residue disposal.
