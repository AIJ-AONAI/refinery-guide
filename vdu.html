Vacuum Distillation Unit (VDU)

The Vacuum Distillation Unit processes the residue from the CDU. Operating under vacuum, it enables distillation of heavier fractions without thermal cracking.

Key Operating Parameters:

  • Temperature: ~400°C
  • Pressure: 25-50 mmHg
  • Main products: Light Vacuum Gas Oil (LVGO), Heavy Vacuum Gas Oil (HVGO)
